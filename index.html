About the Death Note Book

The Death Note is a powerful notebook that has the ability to kill anyone whose name is written inside. So if you want to get rid of your enemy or just someone or something that you don't like, don't hesitate to write their name !! Enjoy being a serial killer and have fun with your own Death Note !
